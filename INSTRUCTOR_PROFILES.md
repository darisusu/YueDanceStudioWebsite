# 悦舞坊简介 | YUE Dance Studio Introduction

## 中文简介
翔悅文艺旗下悅舞坊,自2018年成立以来,始终秉承"传承与创新"的理念,致力于舞蹈艺术的推广与发展。我们汇聚了来自中国内地、台湾和马来西亚的优秀教师团队,团队成员包括经验丰富的舞蹈家、舞蹈教育家,以及充满活力和创造力的年轻教师。

从最初的几位学员到如今的百多人大家庭,悅舞坊见证了无数学员在舞蹈技艺和艺术素养上的成长与蜕变。我们根据学员的不同程度和兴趣,量身定制舞蹈编排,确保每位学员都能在轻松愉悦的学习氛围中,找到属于自己的舞蹈节奏,感受舞蹈带来的快乐与自信。

悦舞坊不仅为学员提供专业的舞蹈培训,更为他们搭建展示才华的舞台。我们定期举办舞台演出,让学员在聚光灯下绽放光彩。同时,我们也积极组织学员参与慈善活动,用舞蹈传递爱与温暖,回馈社会。

悅舞坊深耕华族文化,通过舞蹈这一艺术形式,让更多人感受到华族文化的博大精深和独特魅力。我们相信,舞蹈能够架起沟通的桥梁,让华族文化在多元化的新加坡绽放出更加绚丽的光彩。

"在舞蹈中,遇见更好的自己。"

## English Introduction
Since its establishment in 2018, YUE Dance Studio, a subsidiary of Xiangyue Arts, has been committed to the promotion and development of dance arts while upholding the values of heritage and innovation.

We bring together an outstanding team of teachers from China, Taiwan, and Malaysia with experience in choreography and education of various dance genres.

YUE Dance has grown from just a few students to a family of over a hundred today. We have witnessed the growth and transformation of countless students as they develop their skills and artistic expression through dance.

Our instructors tailor the choreographies according to different skill levels and interests of the students such that everyone can find their rhythm and build confidence in dance, while in pleasant and stress-free learning environment.

Beyond professional dance training, YUE Dance also provides opportunities for students to showcase their talents, such as studio concerts, performances at public events and charity events etc. These are great platforms for our students to shine under the spotlight while sharing the love and joy of dance.

YUE Dance Studio is deeply rooted in Chinese heritage and aims to share the richness and unique charm of Chinese culture through the art of dance. We believe that dance serves as a bridge for the Chinese culture to flourish in the diverse landscape of Singapore.

# 教师团队 | Our Instructors

## 艺术总监 邓宇 (Daniel) 老师 | Artistic Director - Instructor Deng Yu (Daniel)

### 中文简介
Daniel邓宇老师毕业于福建省艺术学院舞蹈系,后在北京舞蹈学院师从现代舞大师曹诚渊先生。曾担任福建省歌舞剧院、香港城市当代舞团首席舞者。1994年移居新加坡,受聘新加坡人民协会舞蹈团;现担任翔悦文艺艺术总监,新加坡南洋艺术学院兼职导师。

1994年获得"中国一级舞蹈演员"称号,曾举办舞蹈专场晚会展示他的作品;多次在国家级、省级舞蹈比赛中获奖:
- 1993年获得中国文化部颁发的最高荣誉奖-文华奖;
- 1992年获中国政府为表彰发展国家文化艺术事业做出突出贡献所颁发的"政府特殊终身津贴"奖;
- 1988代表北京舞蹈学院参加第二届全国"桃李杯"舞蹈比赛,获得现代舞组唯一优秀表演奖及优秀选手的称号。
- 曾代表中国出访美国、日本、朝鲜、巴西等10多个国家;1987被玻利维亚共和国政府授予"杰出外国舞者"称号;1986年获得第二届全国舞蹈比赛表演一等奖。

所取得的成就被录入《中国专家人才库》及《中国当代青年名人大辞典》。在新加坡曾担任多所中学舞蹈指导,编排的舞蹈曾多次在新加坡青年节舞蹈比赛中获得金奖。

Daniel老师拥有近30年的教学经验,为各种程度的舞者编排了专业且引人入胜的课程。他耐心且诚恳,能与学生进行有效的交流,传递的是对舞蹈的欣赏和热爱。采用科学的循序渐进的教学方法,带领学员探索身体的潜能,在自由舒展中找到自我表达的独特语言,舞出属于自己的灵魂节奏。

"舞出人生风格,跳出生命腔调。"

### English Profile
Instructor Daniel graduated from the Dance Department of Fujian Arts Academy and later studied under modern dance master Cao Chengyuan at the Beijing Dance Academy. He served as the principal dancer at the Fujian Opera and Dance Theater and the Hong Kong City Contemporary Dance Company before moving to Singapore in late 1994.

Instructor Daniel has held many solo performances showcasing his works and has won numerous awards in national and provincial-level dance competitions. His achievements have been included in the "China Expert Talent Database" and the "Dictionary of Contemporary Chinese Youth Celebrities."

His numerous accolades include:
- In 1994, he was awarded the prestigious title of China's First-Class Dance Artiste
- In 1993, he received the highest honor awarded by the Ministry of Culture of China-the Wenhua Award.
- In 1992, he was awarded the China National Special Contribution Award by the Chinese government in recognition of his outstanding contributions to the development of national cultural and artistic endeavors.
- In 1988, he represented Beijing Dance Academy in the 2nd National Taoli World Dance Competition and was the sole winner of the Outstanding Performance Award as well as Outstanding Performer in the modern dance category.
- He has represented China in visits to over 10 countries, including the United States, Japan, North Korea, and Brazil. In 1987, the government of Bolivia awarded him the title of "Outstanding Foreign Dancer."
- In 1986, he won First Prize in China's 2nd Nationwide Dance Competition.

In Singapore, he has close to thirty years of teaching experience in his time as dance instructor at various secondary schools and the Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts (NAFA), with many of his choreographies winning gold medals at the Singapore Youth Festival Dance Competitions.

## 民族民间舞 白映文老师 | Ethnic Folk Dance - Instructor Bai Yingwen

### 中文简介
新加坡国际艺术家联盟-主席
新加坡"手拉手国际青少年艺术节"-创办人
中国山西中国舞协会-顾问
中国舞蹈家协会-会员
新加坡淡滨尼艺术团-艺术总监
新加坡东方彩韵国际艺术有限公司-艺术总监

毕业中国北京舞蹈学院民间舞系。1994年协助中国著名舞蹈家贾作光先生建立中国第一间私立舞蹈学校"贾作光舞蹈学校"。1996年受聘新加坡人民协会,担任舞蹈团专业舞员。
1999-2005年受聘担任南洋艺术学院(NAFA)舞蹈系讲师一职。后担任新加坡艺术学校(SOTA)华族舞蹈讲师一职。

舞蹈作品无数,参加过新加坡艺术节,妆艺等大型活动。代表舞蹈作品《走进西藏》,《黑土地的故事》《归》及戏曲晚会"生旦净末丑"等。2002年华族文化节举办"白映文华族舞蹈教学成果展",成为中国境外举办个人舞蹈成果展第一人。

2005年建立东方彩韵国际艺术有限公司,担任新加坡多间中小学舞蹈老师,作品均获新加坡青年节大奖。担任多届马来西亚全国舞蹈节,亚洲舞蹈节,中国小荷风采海外艺术比赛等国际赛事评审。是一位集聚舞蹈表演,教学及编导于一身的艺术家。与本地及周边国家的艺术家和团体合作良好,致力于推广华族舞蹈文化在本地的推广和发展。

白映文老师风趣幽默,课堂上总是充满欢笑声。同时他对每个民族的文化如何融入舞蹈进行细致讲解,使学员在轻松愉快的氛围中掌握技巧,感受民族舞蹈的独特魅力。

"每一个动作,都是时间的诗篇。"

### English Profile
Instructor Bai graduated from the Folk Dance Department of Beijing Dance Academy. In 1994, he assisted the renowned Chinese dancer Mr. Jia Zuoguang in establishing China's first private dance school "Jia Zuoguang Dance School". In 1996, he was hired by the People's Association of Singapore as a professional dancer of the dance company.

He later joined Nanyang Academy of the Arts (NAFA) as a lecturer of the dance department from 1999 to 2005, before serving as a Chinese Dance lecturer at Singapore School of the Arts (SOTA).

Instructor Bai has numerous original choreographies and has participated in large-scale events such as the Singapore Arts Festival and Chingay. Representative dance works include "Entering Tibet", "The Story of Black Land", "Return" and the Chinese Opera "Sheng Dan Jing Mo Chou"("生旦净末丑"). In 2002, the Chinese Cultural Festival held the "Bai Yingwen Chinese Dance Teaching Achievement Exhibition", becoming the first person to hold a personal dance achievement exhibition outside China.

In 2005, Instructor Bai established 东方彩韵国际艺术有限公司 and served as a dance teacher in many primary and secondary schools in Singapore with works that have won first prize in Singapore Youth Festival Awards. He has served as a judge for many international competitions such as the Malaysian National Dance Festival, the Asian Dance Festival, and the China Xiao He Style Overseas Art Competition.

He is an artist who combines dance performance, teaching and choreography. With good connections with artists and groups in local and neighboring countries, Instructor Bai is committed to promoting and developing Chinese dance culture in the local area.

With his wit and humour, Instructor Bai's classes are filled with laughter and joy, yet he also provides detailed explanations on how each ethnic culture is integrated into dance, allowing students to grasp techniques in a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere while experiencing the unique charm of ethnic dance.

## 成人芭蕾舞 沈芷华老师 | Ballet - Instructor Shen Zhi Hua

### 中文简介
沈芷华老师13岁进入中国上海舞蹈学校,1966年以优异成绩毕业于芭蕾舞专科,成为专业芭蕾舞演员。曾任上海芭蕾舞团、《白毛女》剧组演员,出访演出足迹遍及欧美亚多个国家,出演剧目涵盖现代芭蕾、古典芭蕾及中国民族民间舞蹈,代表作包括《红色娘子军》《天鹅湖》《葛佩利亚》等。

**专业经历**
- 1975年起:正式调入上海芭蕾舞团编导组,着重创作工作。这期间的作品参与上海音乐之春演出的有:《青松赞》《海燕》《盼望》《霸王别姬》等作品。
- 1984年:定居新加坡
- 1991年在淡滨尼艺术团担任编导、培训老师、艺术及舞台总监。
- 1992年:成为新加坡公民,先后任教于国家舞蹈团、南方艺术研究会、南洋艺术学院等机构,亦担任多所中小学及CCA舞蹈老师。
- 现任:悅舞坊成人芭蕾舞导师、同时在黄埔CC和淡滨尼CC授课。

**出版作品**
- 2015年新加坡出版长篇小说《开口吧舞鞋》。
- 2016年由中国中科技术大学购买版权,于中国正式发行。

芷华老师教学因材施教,严格而不失关爱,以积极阳光的生活态度感染每一位学员。她对芭蕾的热情深深融入课堂,耐心引导学员感受芭蕾的线条美感与优雅气质。

"生命固舞蹈而灵动,岁月固热爱而闪光。"

### English Profile
Ms. Shen Zhihua began her dance journey at the age of 13 when she was admitted to the Shanghai Dance School in China. She graduated with distinction in 1966, specializing in ballet, and went on to become a professional ballet dancer. She performed with the Shanghai Ballet Company and the "White-Haired Girl" production group, touring extensively across Europe, America, and Asia. Her repertoire includes modern ballet, classical ballet, and Chinese ethnic and folk dance. Notable works she performed in include "The Red Detachment of Women", "Swan Lake", and "Coppélia".

**Professional Experience**
- 1973-1975: Taught at the Shanghai Dance School.
- From 1975: Officially joined the choreographic team of the Shanghai Ballet Company, focusing on dance choreography. Works were featured in Shanghai Spring Music Festival, including "Ode to the Evergreen Pine", "Sea Swallow", "Hope", and "Farewell, My Concubine".
- 1984: Relocated to Singapore
- 1991: Joined Tampines Arts Troupe as choreographer, dance trainer, and Artistic & Stage Director.
- 1992: Obtained Singapore citizenship; taught at the National Dance Company, Southern Arts Research Association, Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts, and various primary and secondary schools.
- Current Roles: Adult Ballet Instructor at Yue Dance Workshop (悦舞坊), instructor at Whampoa CC, Tampines CC.

**Publications**
- In 2015, published the novel "Dancing Shoes Speak" in Singapore.
- In 2016, the copyright was acquired by the University of Science and Technology of China, and the novel was officially published in China.

Instructor Shen tailors her teaching to each student, maintaining a firm yet caring approach. She inspires every student with her positivity and integrates her deep passion for ballet in each class while patiently guiding students to appreciate the beauty of ballet's elegant charm.

### 中文简介
黄卓沁老师来自柔佛古来加拉巴沙威,毕业于南京艺术学院中国民族民间舞表演专业本科,北京舞蹈学院中国民族民间舞等级考试1至6级教师资格证,大学毕业后就职于新加坡华族舞蹈刷场担任演员一职。

曾荣获马来西亚全国华族舞蹈公开赛独舞冠军及群舞冠军等奖项,亚洲舞蹈艺术节金奖及编导奖,新加坡金狮杯国际音乐艺术节金奖等。编创三人藏族舞《藏·彌》,独舞《在远方》荣获金奖及编导奖,双人傣族舞《迷雾》,三人维吾尔族舞《三友》等,参与中国身韵巡礼基础教材和高级教材及角色塑造教材学习,非遗文化继承人专家课汉族东北秧歌,花鼓灯,维族,傣族,侗族,苗族等。

卓沁老师年轻富有活力,不仅专业功底深厚,教学细致耐心,还与学员亦师亦友,深受大家喜爱。

"舞蹈让您遇见更美的自己。"

### English Profile
A passion that began back in her hometown of Kulai, Malaysia led young Zhuo Qin Wong to pursue Chinese Dance professionally in China. She graduated with a bachelor's degree in Chinese Ethnic and Folk Dance Performance from Nanjing University of the Arts and holds Levels 1 to 6 Teaching Certification in Chinese Ethnic and Folk Dance from Beijing Dance Academy. Upon graduation she joined the Singapore Chinese Dance Theatre as a dancer, performing in various company productions internationally.

She has won numerous awards, including the Solo and Group Dance Championships at the Malaysian National Chinese Dance Open Competition, the Gold Award and Choreography Award at the Asian Dance Arts Festival, and the Gold Award at the Singapore Golden Lion Cup International Music and Arts Festival.

Her choreographed works, such as the trio Tibetan dance "Zang Mi" and the solo dance "In the Distance", have received Gold Awards and Choreography Awards. Other notable works include the duet Dai dance "Misty Fog" and the trio Uyghur dance "Three Friends".

Instructor Zhuo Qin has also participated in training programs for foundational and advanced levels of Shen Yun movement studies and role development, as well as expert courses in various cultural dance forms such as Han Chinese Northeast Yangko dance, Anhui's folk dance form Huagudeng, Uyghur, Dai, Dong, and Miao minority ethnic dances.

Instructor Zhuo Qin strives to impart a strong foundation of dance to her students. Her patient yet meticulous guidance is well received by her students, who also see her as a trusted friend.
